Corona support

At Corona Labs, we want you to be successful with our products. There will, of course,
be times where you have specific questions and we want you to have access to the answers you need.
As such, we offer two levels of support: standard support and priority support.

Standard support

Corona Labs offers comprehensive documentation, guides, and tutorials which can answer almost any question you may have.

If you need more specific assistance, please visit the Corona community forums or public Slack channel - great places to get answers from experienced Corona developers and from the Corona Labs Developer Relations team.

Priority support

If you need additional support, a priority support plan may be the answer. With priority support, you get closer access to our team of specialists.
